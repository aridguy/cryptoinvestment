About Us Title...

Shunwei Investment and Capital Management is a specialist global alternatives investment manager with unique competitive advantages in e-financials,precious metals and stones, sports wagering, Blockchain, AI, machine learning, bio-tech, and healthcare; with a focus on quality growth companies and also an expert in recommending trusted business opportunities for their clients. Shunwei Investment and Capital Mnagement was founded in 2013 by Maxwell Zhao, who was previously with, UBS and Merrill Lynch during his +32 year career in financial services and management. The Shunwei Investment and Management Capital team is based in Kuala Lumpur and the company is licensed as an asset manager by the Malaysian Financial Ministry and is majority-owned by the founder and team; and minority-owned by Limited Partner investors who share a collective history together and support wider industry reach and expertise altogether. Our Philosophy and Approach We firmly believe that the Financial Services and Technology industries are in the beginning phase of an unprecedented financial revolution.
