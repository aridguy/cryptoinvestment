Meet The Team

Daniel Wong
Finance Manager

Daniel joined Shunwei in 2019 and has more than 25 years of experience in Finance and Accounting in various listed group companies including Hong Leong Group and ICBC Group.

Before joining Shunwei Investment and Capital Management, Daniel was the Operations and Finance Manager for a Hedge Fund Manager for 6 years. Prior to that, he worked with ICBC Group’s Asset Management division as Operations and Finance Manager for over 7 years. Daniel holds a Masters Degree of Professional Accounting from The University of Malaya. He is a profound member of the Association of International Accountants.

Kevin Lo
Business Analyst

Kevin joined Shunwei Investment and Management Capital as a Business Analyst in early 2018 and while his coverage is general in nature, has a particular interest in Blockchain technology and Crypto currency.

Prior to joining Shunwei Investment and Management Capital, Kevin was at Overseas-Chinese Banking Corporation (OCBC) where he advised High Net-worth clients on financial markets and investments for two years. Previous to OCBC, Kevin worked in Trade Finance and Accounting at Petronas. Kevin holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (Honors) from the University at Buffalo.

Michael Hurst
Senior Analyst

Michael brings 22 years of experience in Financial markets, as both an Analyst and as a Portfolio Manager. He has particular expertise in the Financial Institutions,Crypto Markets and Healthcare sectors.

Before joining Shunwei Investment and Management Capital, Michael held several roles at Magellan Asset Management, including Global Fund Portfolio Manager and Head of Healthcare. Prior to Magellan, he spent 9 years at Citigroup in investment banking, with a focus on Financial Institutions and sponsor M&A coverage. Michael holds a PhD in Finance and a Bachelor of Commerce (First Class Honours), both from the University of Sydney.
